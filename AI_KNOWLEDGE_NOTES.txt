AI Knowledge + Schedule + Events
--------------------------------
New files:
  src/ai/knowledge.js
  src/ai/schedule.js
  src/ai/events.js
  src/ai/router.v2.js
  src/ai/boot-demo.js  (optional helper)

Use `respondToV2(...)` from router.v2.js to enable:
- Relationship & world facts
- Schedule with meeting overrides (reverts to normal afterward automatically)
- Pre-planned events that flip flags/behaviors and bump meters
- Location-based verbosity (e.g., coffee shop => concise Q&A)

Quick test in console after loading your game:
  await window.AI.respondV2({ id:'lily', name:'Lily', relations:{ MC:{ type:'sister', strength:80 } } }, "Hey Lily, are you coming to the coffee shop?")

To integrate permanently, call `respondToV2` from your chat handler instead of the old router.
